#  Hangman Game - Special Edition 

A graphical Hangman game with a special romantic message! **Play instantly in your browser - just click the link!** 

## Features

- **Play Instantly**: No download, no installation - just click and play!
- **Web-Based**: Works on any device with a browser (phone, tablet, computer)
- **Type or Click**: Use keyboard or click letter buttons
- **Visual Hangman Drawing**: Progressive hangman figure as you make wrong guesses
- **Bonus Life Feature**: Get a special Lithuanian message when you're about to lose!

## How to Play

1. Type letters A-Z on your keyboard to make guesses
2. Try to figure out the hidden word before the hangman is complete
3. You get 6 wrong guesses, but here's the twist...
4. When you reach 6 wrong guesses, you get a special bonus message and the counter resets!
5. **Keep playing until you guess the complete answer!** 🎉
